SWIFT designates **Category 7** messages strictly for Documentary Credits and Guarantees. To support the end-to-end Import LC lifecycle we defined in the BRD, your system must generate and parse several other critical MT messages.

Here is the detailed specification for the additional SWIFT messages required to manage Amendments, Document Presentations, Discrepancies, and Settlements.

**IMPORTANT NOTES:** One of the highest causes of Straight-Through Processing (STP) failures in Trade Finance is when internal system data does not strictly conform to SWIFT's rigid formatting rules, resulting in a network NACK (Negative Acknowledgement).

To ensure the business requirements translate flawlessly into system UI validations, the tables below have been updated with a dedicated **SWIFT Validation & Constraints** column. 

***

### Global SWIFT Validation Rules (Applies to all messages)
Before detailing specific fields, the system UI must globally enforce these SWIFT standards:
* **The SWIFT "X" Character Set:** Most Category 7 fields strictly allow only the following characters: `A-Z`, `a-z`, `0-9`, and `- / ? : ( ) . , ' +` and spaces. The system must block or auto-convert invalid characters (like `@`, `&`, `_`, or `#`) upon data entry.
* **No Invalid Slashes:** No reference field can start or end with a slash `/`, nor can it contain two consecutive slashes `//`.
* **Decimal Format:** Financial amounts must use a comma `,` as the decimal separator, not a period. 

***

### 1. MT 701: Issue of a Documentary Credit (Continuation)
**Purpose:** To continue the text of an MT 700 if the data exceeds SWIFT's strict character limits (specifically for the narrative tags like Description of Goods or Documents Required).
**Usage / Condition:** Automatically generated by the system during **Process 3.1 (Issuance)** *only if* tags 45A, 46A, or 47A in the MT 700 exceed 100 lines of 65 characters.
**Data Mapping:**

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **27** | Sequence of Total | M | Mapped to `2/2` or `2/3`. | Format: `Digit/Digit`. Max 5 characters. |
| **20** | Documentary Credit No. | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. Must exactly match Tag 20 of the parent MT 700. |
| **45B** | Description of Goods | O | Overflow text from MT 700 Tag 45A. | Max 100 lines of 65 characters per line. Must strictly adhere to the X Character Set. |
| **46B** | Documents Required | O | Overflow text from MT 700 Tag 46A. | Max 100 lines of 65 characters per line. X Character Set. |
| **47B** | Additional Conditions | O | Overflow text from MT 700 Tag 47A. | Max 100 lines of 65 characters per line. X Character Set. |

### 2. MT 707: Amendment to a Documentary Credit
**Purpose:** To inform the Advising Bank (and Beneficiary) of changes to the terms of an issued LC.
**Usage / Condition:** Generated during **Process 3.2 (Amendments)** when a Checker authorizes an amendment. 
**Data Mapping:**
*Note: In an MT 707, you only populate the fields that are actually changing.*

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **20** | Sender's Reference | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. No invalid slashes. |
| **21** | Receiver's Reference | M | Advising Bank's Ref (or `NONREF`) | Max 16 characters. No invalid slashes. |
| **31C** | Date of Issue | M | Original `Issue Date` | Exact format: `YYMMDD`. Cannot be a future date. |
| **30** | Date of Amendment | M | `LcAmendment.amendmentDate` | Exact format: `YYMMDD`. |
| **32B** | Increase of LC Amount | O | `LcAmendment.amountIncrease` | 3-letter ISO Currency + Amount (Max 15 digits including comma). E.g., `USD50000,00`. |
| **33B** | Decrease of LC Amount | O | `LcAmendment.amountDecrease` | 3-letter ISO Currency + Amount. Same as 32B. |
| **34B** | New LC Amount | O | `ImportLc.newTotalAmount` | 3-letter ISO Currency + Amount. Must equal Original +/- Amendment. |
| **31E** | New Expiry Date | O | `LcAmendment.newExpiryDate` | Exact format: `YYMMDD`. |
| **79** | Narrative (Amendment Details)| O | `LcAmendment.narrativeText` | Max 35 lines of 50 characters. Uses the "Z" Character Set (allows wider range of characters like `@`, `#`, `=`). |

### 3. MT 750: Advice of Discrepancy
**Purpose:** To notify the Presenting Bank that documents contain discrepancies, and that the Issuing Bank is contacting the Applicant for a waiver. It effectively pauses the SLA timer.
**Usage / Condition:** Generated during **Process 3.3 (Document Examination)** when a Checker marks a presentation as *Discrepant* and seeks Applicant instructions.

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **20** | Sender's Reference | M | `PresentationRecord.presentationRef` | Max 16 characters. No invalid slashes. |
| **21** | Related Reference | M | `PresentationRecord.presentingBankRef`| Max 16 characters. |
| **32B** | Principal Amount Claimed | M | `PresentationRecord.claimAmount` | 3-letter ISO Currency + Amount (Max 15 digits). |
| **77J** | Discrepancies | M | `PresentationRecord.discrepancyDetails`| Max 70 lines of 50 characters. X Character Set. System must auto-wrap discrepancy text to fit this constraint. |
| **72Z** | Sender to Receiver Info | O | Standard text indicating waiver status. | Max 6 lines of 35 characters. Can include SWIFT code words (e.g., `/TELEMAC/`). |

### 4. MT 734: Notice of Refusal
**Purpose:** The ultimate legal rejection. It informs the Presenting Bank that the documents are discrepant and the Issuing Bank officially refuses to honor the claim.
**Usage / Condition:** Generated during **Process 3.3** if the Applicant explicitly *refuses* to waive the discrepancies, or if the bank refuses the presentation due to severe risk/compliance violations.

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **20** | Sender's Reference | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. |
| **21** | Presenting Bank's Ref | M | `PresentationRecord.presentingBankRef`| Max 16 characters. |
| **32A** | Value Date & Amount | M | `PresentationRecord.claimAmount` | Format: `YYMMDD` + 3-letter CCY + Amount. Value date must be the presentation date or current date. |
| **73** | Charges Deducted | O | Calculated discrepancy fees. | Max 6 lines of 35 characters. X Character Set. |
| **77J** | Discrepancies | M | `PresentationRecord.discrepancyDetails`| Max 70 lines of 50 characters. X Character Set. |
| **77B** | Disposal of Documents | M | Enum logic (e.g., "HOLDING DOCUMENTS"). | Max 3 lines of 35 characters. Must clearly state the legal status of the physical documents. |

| **20** | Documentary Credit No. | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. |
| **21** | Presenting Bank's Ref | M | `PresentationRecord.presentingBankRef`| Max 16 characters. |
| **30** | Date of Advice | M | System Business Date | Exact format: `YYMMDD`. |
| **32B** | Amount Advised | M | `PresentationRecord.acceptedAmount` | 3-letter ISO Currency + Amount (Max 15 digits). |
| **72Z** | Sender to Receiver Info | O | "DISCREPANCIES WAIVED..." | Max 6 lines of 35 characters. |

### 6. MT 732: Advice of Discharge
**Purpose:** Used strictly for Usance (Deferred Payment) LCs. It serves as a formal acknowledgment that clean documents were received and the Issuing Bank commits to paying on the exact maturity date.
**Usage / Condition:** Generated automatically during **Process 3.4 (Settlement & Payment)** when a Usance presentation enters the *Accepted* state, long before the actual money moves.

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **20** | Sender's Reference | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. |
| **21** | Presenting Bank's Ref | M | `PresentationRecord.presentingBankRef`| Max 16 characters. |
| **30** | Date of Advice | M | System Business Date | Exact format: `YYMMDD`. |
| **32A** | Value Date & Amount | M | `PresentationRecord.maturityDate` + `Accepted Amount` | Format: `YYMMDD` + 3-letter CCY + Amount. The date here *must* be the future maturity date legally agreed upon. |

### 7. MT 799: Free Format Message
**Purpose:** A generic, unformatted message used for trade queries, tracers, or specific instructions that do not fit into the structured formats above.
**Usage / Condition:** In our BRD, this is generated during **Process 3.6 (Cancellations)** to request the Beneficiary's mutual consent to cancel an LC prior to expiry.

| SWIFT Tag | Description | M/O | System Source / Data Extraction Logic | SWIFT Validation & Constraints |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **20** | Transaction Reference | M | `TradeInstrument.transactionRef` | Max 16 characters. |
| **21** | Related Reference | M | Advising Bank's Ref (or `NONREF`) | Max 16 characters. |
| **79** | Narrative | M | Generated by system rule for cancellations. | Max 35 lines of 50 characters. Z Character Set. System must enforce line wrapping. |

### 8. The Settlement Messages (Category 1 & 2)
While Category 7 handles the *data* and *rules* of Trade Finance, actual money moves via Category 1 or 2 messages. These are generated during **Process 3.4 (Settlement & Payment)** upon final execution.

* **MT 202 (General Financial Institution Transfer):** Used when the Issuing Bank is transferring funds bank-to-bank to reimburse the Presenting Bank via correspondent Nostro/Vostro accounts.
    * *Key Fields:* `20` (Reference), `32A` (Value Date/Amount), `53a` (Sender's Correspondent), `58a` (Beneficiary Institution - The Presenting Bank).
* **MT 103 (Single Customer Credit Transfer):** Used less frequently in Trade Finance, but occasionally utilized if the Issuing Bank is instructed to pay the foreign supplier (Beneficiary) directly into their specific retail bank account, bypassing bank-to-bank reimbursement flows.
