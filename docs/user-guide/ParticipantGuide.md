# Participant Operating Guide: Digital Trade Finance

Welcome to the Digital Trade Finance platform. This guide covers the core operations for Bank Participants.

## 1. Instrument Issuance (Maker Role)
1. Navigate to the **Issuance Data Entry** stepper.
2. Complete all 4 data steps (Instrument Details, Documents Required, Clauses, Limits).
3. On Step 5 (**Final Review**), click **Submit for Authorization**.
4. The transaction will move to the **Checker Queue**.

## 2. Transaction Approval (Checker Role)
1. Open the **Checker Authorization Dashboard**.
2. Review the **Risk Matrix** (Limit Compliance, Sanctions, Discrepancies).
3. Enter optional comments and click **Approve Transaction**.
> [!NOTE]
> The platform prevents "Self-Authorization". You cannot approve instruments you created.

## 3. Shipping Guarantees
- Use the Shipping Guarantee module when goods arrive before documents.
- The system automatically secures **110%** of the invoice amount against the customer's facility.
