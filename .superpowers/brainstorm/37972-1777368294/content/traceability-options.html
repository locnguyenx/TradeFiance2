Designing Transaction & Instrument Traceability

How should users interact with Transactions vs. the underlying Instrument?

A

Unified Timeline

Chronological feed of all events (Create LC, Authorized, Amended, Presented). Best for seeing the story of the instrument.

B

Tabbed Navigation

Separate "Overview" (Aggregate Data) from "History" (Individual Transactions). Best for complex LCs with many amendments.

C

Active Hero

The latest transaction is pinned to the top as a "Workflow Hero". Best for action-oriented users focusing on current task.
